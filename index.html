Welcome to NC Data4Good

Thanks and welcome to the NC Data4Good page on Github. Our mission is to bring together the community of data professionals to make an impact to local communities by using data science for social good.

Data Crunch for Social Good

We have organized a 'Data Crunch for Social Good' event November 7 & 8, 2015 to benefit the United Way of the Greater Triangle. Check out the Resources & Data pages.
